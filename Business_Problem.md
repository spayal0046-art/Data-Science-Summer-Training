# Project 1: Business Problem Framing

## Problem Title
Customer Churn Prediction

## Business Problem
Many telecom companies lose customers every month because customers switch to other service providers. This reduces company revenue and increases customer acquisition costs.

## Business Objective
The objective is to identify customers who are likely to leave the company so that the company can take preventive actions and improve customer retention.

## Required Data
- Customer ID
- Age
- Gender
- Monthly Charges
- Total Charges
- Contract Type
- Internet Service
- Tenure
- Churn (Yes/No)

## Machine Learning Problem Statement
This is a Classification problem where the target variable is "Churn" (Yes/No).

## Expected Output
- Predict whether a customer will leave or not.
- Identify high-risk customers.
- Help the business improve customer retention.

## Business Benefits
- Increase customer retention
- Reduce revenue loss
- Improve customer satisfaction
- Support better business decisions
